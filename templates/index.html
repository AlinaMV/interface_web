The satirical BART is a model specifically crafted to turn regular sentences into satirical ones. Trained on a diverse dataset in english and in french that includes satire examples, the model learns to recognize linguistic patterns and contextual cues for humor. By applying this knowledge, it skillfully transforms ordinary sentences into witty, satirical expressions, infusing them with irony and cleverness.
